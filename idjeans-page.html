Custom your jeans

Almost everyone has the experience of buying jeans. However, it's not easy to buyjeans which perfectly fit our body. Collaborating with a merchant from Taobao, we built a website for customing jeans.

Competitive analysis

We search many different websites for custom-made jeans on the Internet. We found that these websites can be categorized into two kinds::

1. Create jeans from the beginning. Most people aren't designer, it's hard for them to create a pair of jeans considering every details.

2. Show jeans but no custom option. Such kind of custom websites are copied from E-Shop website which is good at showing the details of jeans but actually not appropriated as custom-made since it contains no custom options and body measurement input.

Custom jeans with as little option as possible.
