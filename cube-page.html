Learn how to solve the Rubik's Cube

I had been frustrated by the Rubik's Cube until I entered my college when I learned to solve it. However, learning how to solve the Rubik's Cube is boring because it requires so much patience and practice.

We can find tutorials on the Internet which just show what to do step by step.

I don't like such tutorial because I was often interrupted during the long steps, and I have to do this again from the beginning.

After practicing from day and night, I can finally solve the Rubik's Cube but still, I don't know how these steps come and why doing these steps.

There must be a better way.

As I think about it, a better way should have such advantages:

1. After being interrupted I can continue from current status.

2. More explainations, letting me learn how and why.

Use an iPad

I want to simulate a Rubik's Cube in iPad because by using multi-touch screen people can easily interact with the cube. My team also designs an Expert System to show action guide and text explanation which, of course, base on current status of the cube.
